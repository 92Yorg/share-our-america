Why It Works

  • This toolkit helps leaders implement the Share Our [Organization] model, which is rooted in Reflective Structured Dialogue (RSD), a research-based framework developed by Essential Partners. Direct outcomes of RSD include improved belonging, mutual understanding, and trust, even across significant differences. In an organization, RSD can mend relationships and establish healthier communication norms. It also sets the stage for successful strategic planning, policy change, and culture initiatives, while unlocking the potential of diverse experiences and identities. When implemented thoughtfully, these dialogue sessions elevate the voices of your members, improve trust and understanding within and between teams, and open a pathway to change. Whether your goal is to use this model to respond to a tense moment for your team members, embark on a major project, or gather ideas towards building a new strategy, you’ll be able to move forward after the sessions to integrate the diverse, emergent perspectives into your organization.

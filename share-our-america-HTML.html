  • Share Our America is a one-of-a-kind domestic cultural exchange program bringing Americans of different backgrounds closer together.

    Participants will travel to parts of the U.S. they haven’t been exposed to and explore life through a local perspective, building towards greater empathy and understanding.

Are you concerned that polarization in America is reaching a crisis point? Do you feel that people are growing further apart and becoming increasingly isolated in their politics and culture? Does it seem that as a society we will never be able to address the most pressing problems — from climate change to racial and economic justice to health to education — unless we can find some way to bridge our divides?

SHARE OUR AMERICA, a new initiative of 92Y’s Belfer Center for Innovation and Social Impact, is founded on the idea that change can happen through finding common ground — and that begins on a one-to-one basis — person by person, community by community. SHARE OUR AMERICA is a new domestic cultural exchange program, designed to bring diverse and disparate individuals together to share their viewpoints and better understand each other’s worldviews — with the goal of fostering understanding, respect, and empathy.

Join us — and start making a real difference. Our first project brings 92Y patrons on a trip to an interdenominational church community in rural Nebraska. Over the course of four days and three nights in the town of Chadron — an agricultural community in the picturesque Pine Ridge in the western part of the state — participants will stay with local hosts and explore life through this community’s unique perspective. Travelers will come away with new insights about our country and its rich diversity of perspectives, a new experience of rural American life, and new friendships with people they otherwise would not have met.

About

Developed by the Belfer Center for Innovation & Social Impact at The 92nd Street Y, New York, Share Our America in an initiative co-designed with communities across our United States. We are a growing community of people and organizations who believe that we can bridge the vast divides that exist in our politics and our culture by meaningfully connecting with each other as humans.

Using research-backed strategies to reduce polarization, Share Our America brings Americans of different backgrounds together to share their viewpoints and better understand each other's worldviews — with the goal of fostering greater empathy, understanding, respect and trust. Through Share Our America gatherings, participants and community members connect over shared meals, shared experiences, through social events, and facilitated conversation about our differences.

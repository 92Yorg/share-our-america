About

Share Our America is a national initiative to reduce polarization in our country, bringing Americans from different backgrounds together to understand each other's perspectives, and build empathy, respect, and trust.

  • Share Our America experiences are rooted in local communities, co-designed and led by community organizations and leaders across the United States. These leaders believe that change can happen in our country — and that begins on a one-to-one basis — person by person, community by community. During Share Our America experiences, participants connect through shared meals, local activities, social events, and facilitated conversations about their differences.

Share Our America was developed by the Belfer Center for Innovation & Social Impact at The 92nd Street Y, New York (92NY) using research-backed strategies to reduce polarization. It was created in close collaboration with industry experts in cultural exchange and building connection across difference.

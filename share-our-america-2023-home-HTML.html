Rebuilding trust and reducing polarization — person by person, community by community.

Share Our America is a national initiative — rooted in local communities — that brings Americans together across our differences to build empathy, understanding, respect, and trust.

See the impact

  • Americans are ready to bridge divides.

    Record-high levels of fear, distrust, and polarization threaten the frayed fabric of American society — yet 86% of Americans believe we can improve our trust in one another (Pew Research, 2019). Share Our America is a model built from these beliefs to recognize the power in difference, resolve conflict, and reduce polarization. It’s time we unite together for a stronger future — and the first step is to talk to each other.

  • Let’s come back together.

    Share Our America is a growing network of people and organizations across the nation who believe that we can bridge our differences in politics, belonging, safety, religion, and more. We support those with an urge to understand others — from local leaders to trained facilitators — who want to bridge the divides they see across their communities.
